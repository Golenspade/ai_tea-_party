I need exploring the concept of "Spatial UI". How does it differ from traditional flat web design?

Spatial UI breaks the boundaries of the traditional 2D screen by introducing depth, translucency, and physical-like interaction models. Instead of stacking flat rectangles, you are layering glass-like materials in a 3D space.

Key Characteristics:
  • Heavy use of background blur to establish depth (z-axis).
  • Vibrant, moving ambient lighting behind elements.
  • Subtle shadows serving as elevation indicators, not just decoration.
