Meeting with The Critic

TUESDAY, 10:45 AM

How do we balance the negative space in an editorial interface without making it feel empty or unfinished?
C.

Negative space is not emptiness; it is the structure that supports the content. Think of it as the silence between musical notes. Without silence, there is only noise.

To avoid the feeling of an unfinished page, the tension between elements must be calculated. The margins must relate proportionally to the column width, and the typography must have enough weight to anchor the eye.
