Query // User
How does the concept of brutalist architecture translate into modern web interfaces?
Ar.
Response // Architect
Brutalism in web design, much like its architectural counterpart, exposes the underlying structure. It rejects forced ornamentation in favor of raw utility.

It manifests through monospaced typography, stark grid layouts, pure high-contrast colors, and a focus on content over decorative containers. You are looking at a mild abstraction of it right now.
